The International Symposium on Computational Structure Prediction and Advanced Materials (ISCSP&AM) brings together experts in science and technology to share the latest breakthroughs in discovering new materials. The symposium will feature presentations and discussions on cutting-edge theoretical and experimental advances, focusing on novel computational methods for predicting material structures and their applications in uncovering electrides, transparent conducting oxides (TCOs), superconductors, and other electronic materials.

The 1st ISCSP&AM was held in Xi'an, China from October 9 to 12, 2019. The 2nd ISCSP&AM also took place in Xi'an, China from August 3 to 4, 2023.
